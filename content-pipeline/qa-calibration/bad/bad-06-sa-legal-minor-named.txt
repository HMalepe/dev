A family in a small town outside Polokwane became the subject of a police investigation after a domestic incident escalated to a fatality.

The household included two adults and their fourteen-year-old daughter, Lindiwe Maluleke, who was reportedly the only witness to what happened that night. According to neighbours, Lindiwe told a family friend what she saw before police arrived, though she has not yet testified in any formal proceeding.

The mechanism at the centre of the case, per early police statements, was a single fall down a stairwell, though the injury pattern described by first responders raised questions that investigators are still working through.

The unraveling of the timeline so far rests almost entirely on what Lindiwe is said to have told that family friend, since no other witnesses have come forward.

The case remains in its early stages, with investigators expected to formally interview Lindiwe in the coming weeks as the matter proceeds through the courts.
