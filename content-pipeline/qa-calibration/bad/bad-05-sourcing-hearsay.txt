Somewhere along the coast near Gqeberha, people say a fisherman vanished under strange circumstances a few years back. The details are a bit fuzzy, but locals still talk about it.

From what's been reported, he was middle-aged and had been out on his boat that morning like usual. Some say his boat was found drifting, though other accounts claim it washed up on the rocks instead — either way, he was never seen again.

As for what actually happened, it's widely believed that he probably ran into some kind of trouble out at sea, maybe engine failure, maybe something else entirely. A few people online have suggested there might have been foul play involved, possibly connected to a dispute he supposedly had with another boat owner, though nothing about that has ever really been confirmed.

Eventually, it seems like the investigation just quietly fizzled out, and nobody really knows the current status of the case anymore.
